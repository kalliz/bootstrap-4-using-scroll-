Mi A3: The best of Google and best of Xiaomi come together.
Mi A3 features impressive hardware and innovations from Google to offer you the complete smartphone experience. Featuring an unmatched Smart AI Triple camera setup of 48MP + 8MP + 2MP on the rear, this smartphone delivers clearly detailed pictures, no matter what the scene. The distinctly superior Super AMOLED display allows you to view images, videos and more in high definition. Running on the Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 655, you have the speed and convenience you need for any smartphone activity.
Mi A3 Features/ Specifications: - The 48MP primary camera features a Sony IMX586 sensor for crystal clear pictures - The 8MP rear camera with 118° FOV enables you to capture ultra-wide portraits with ease - The third 2MP camera functions as a depth sensor for crystal clear detection of far off subjects in any scene - The Super AMOLED Display delivers a big bang entertainment experience with the wide colour gamut and richly lucid detailing - Work through the day with the 4030mAh battery that has 18W Fast Charge capability - A seamlessly fast operation is guaranteed with the efficient Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 655 processor
